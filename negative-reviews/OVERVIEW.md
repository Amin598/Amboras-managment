# Negative Reviews — Research Vault

This folder collects raw negative reviews, complaints, and frustrations from Shopify merchants and users of related tools (apps, page builders, email platforms, etc.).

## Purpose

These reviews are the foundation of Amboras' product thesis:

> Shopify gives you the tools but doesn't guarantee your sales or help you make sales.
> Every tool is slow, not AI-native, and requires a separate app for every basic task.

We mine these reviews to:
- Identify the most painful, recurring problems
- Find gaps that no existing tool solves well
- Validate Amboras features before building them
- Sharpen our positioning and messaging

## What to Upload Here

- Screenshots or text dumps of App Store reviews (Shopify App Store, G2, Trustpilot, Reddit)
- Twitter/X complaints threads
- YouTube comment sections from Shopify tutorial videos
- Facebook group posts
- Founder interviews or podcast quotes
- Any raw, unfiltered merchant frustration

Name files descriptively:
- `shopify-app-store-reviews-[topic].md`
- `reddit-[subreddit]-[topic].md`
- `trustpilot-shopify-[date].md`
- `twitter-complaints-[topic].md`

## Key Themes to Watch For

- AI limitations / no AI-native workflows
- App overload (needing 5+ apps for basic tasks)
- Slow store performance caused by apps
- Poor description / copywriting tools
- Sales not guaranteed — just infrastructure
- Support quality
- Pricing / transaction fees
- Migration difficulty
- Checkout customization limits
- Analytics that don't drive action
